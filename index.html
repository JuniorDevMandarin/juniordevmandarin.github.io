Why Minicoders?

Motivation

Kids want to play, and they want to play in today’s platforms. Minicoders offers game experiences in popular metaverses, like Roblox and Minecraft, so kids grow curious to learn about computational thinking while enjoying the platforms they love the most.

For everyone

Inclusiveness in a broad sense. We are commited to creating a learning environment that is welcoming and inclusive, genderless and accessible even to students with ADHD and Dyslexia.

Educational Standards

Our program follows the CSTA standards for K-12 level teaching, which are widely recognized as a gold standard in computer science education. As well as complying with indications by ISTE and The National Curriculum in England.

Teacher guides

Educators are provided with confortable guides, and all the tools and resources they need to succeed in their role.

Teacher support

An open channel of continuous support for teachers so that they can prepare and conduct classes smoothly.

Automatic Assesment

Save time and effort evaluating, students get certified and automated assesment through the game experiences and activities.
